Guest Wifi

For Guest WiFi we provide managed wifi in hotels for guests to use. We use Cisco Meraki, Aruba, Rukus and Cambium managed WiFi systems.

IPTV

For IPTV we are the Regional partner for otrum.com which is a cloud based hospitality TV management platform and Guest Casting solution that integrates with hotel management software, we also partner with Samsung and LG for the supply of hospitality TV's.

Internet and voice

We provide internet services to hotels and dense user environments and offer a best in class end user experience by implementing QoS and caching strategies that help a single internet feed cope with the number of users in a hospitality environment.

This QoS also caters for guest IP voice services whether that be local, regional or international voice call termination. We understand the hospitality environment and can provider a PABX and call termination solution to suit... hospitality telephony products we supply are Alcatel and NEC.

Remote Accommodation camps and Crew welfare

It's basically supply the hospitality services but to workers in mining / construction and camps and on ships. Keeping them same with voice / video contact to their family's and the outside world.

Back of House infrastructure and systems

Backend solutions is the networks that hotels etc use to check in their guests and is etc.
